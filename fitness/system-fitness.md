## Progressive overload approach

basically my worflow is as follows:

- I start at about 6-7 reps for a weight.
- I try to improve every session (e.g. I might do 3 sets of 7,7,7 reps this week and then main for 8,8,8 the next week etc) for every given exercise. Im not saying I make gains every session but thats the aim
- As my reps move higher to 12-13 range, I move up in weigh for that execise
- Note that for some muscles (e.g. legs I like to experiment with higher reps like 15-20 before moving on)
- Rinse and repeat

As explained in my split, I have a 5 day split. If I feel sore or am busy with work/uni I might leave 1 rest day between any 2 days.
